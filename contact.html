Contact us

Call Us

For questions about closures or construction activities, call our 24-hr project construction hotlines:

  • For Montlake Project construction, call 206-775-8885 (24-hr / call or text)
  • For SR 520/I-5 Connection Project construction, call 206-316-2559 (24-hr / call or text)
  • For general SR 520 inquiries, call 206-200-9484 (8-5, Mon-Fri)
  • To contact our ombudsman, David Goldberg, call 202-412-7930
